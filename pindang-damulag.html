Pindang Damulag

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs carabao meat (or beef)
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 4 cups water

Time

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2-3 hours
  • Total Time: 2.25-3.25 hours
  • Servings: 4-6

Procedure

  1. Cure Meat: Marinate meat in vinegar and salt for 1-2 days.
  2. Cook: Rinse meat and place in a pot with garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water.
  3. Simmer: Cook over low heat until meat is tender, about 2-3 hours.
  4. Season: Season with salt and pepper.
