LENGUA

Ingredients

  • 1 ox tongue
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup mushrooms (optional)
  • 4 cups beef or chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Time

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2-3 hours (boiling)
  • 30 minutes (sautéing and simmering)
  • Total Time: 2.5-3.5 hours
  • Servings: 6-8

Procedure

  1. Boil Tongue: Place the ox tongue in a pot of water and boil for 2-3 hours until tender. Remove, cool, and peel off the skin. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Sauté: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Cook: Add the sliced tongue to the pan and cook until slightly browned.
  4. Simmer: Add bay leaves and broth. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add Vegetables: Add diced carrots, potatoes, and mushrooms. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
