Ube Halaya

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and mashed ube (purple yam)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Time

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30-40 minutes
  • Total Time: 40-50 minutes
  • Servings: 6-8

Procedure

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a pan, combine mashed ube, coconut milk, condensed milk, butter, and sugar.
  2. Cook: Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thick and creamy.
  3. Cool: Pour into a dish and let cool before serving.
