Welcome to Cuisinang Capampangan: Manyaman!

Hello, I'm Kimberly, and this website is my little corner of the internet. I created Cuisinang Capampangan: Manyaman for everyone—students, men, and women alike—who are interested in learning how to cook traditional Kapampangan dishes and desserts.

The name Cuisinang Capampangan translates to "Kapampangan kitchen" in Tagalog, and manyaman is a Kapampangan word meaning "delicious." As a proud Kapampangan, we say it with passion: "Manyaman!" when describing the delightful flavors of our cuisine.

I was inspired to create this website because I am a Kapampangan, born and raised in San Fernando, Pampanga. I have always had a deep appreciation for our local dishes, and I love to indulge in the flavors of our own culinary heritage. This website aims to be a helpful resource for those who need quick and easy access to Kapampangan recipes and ingredients.
