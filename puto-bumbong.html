Puto Bumbong

Ingredients

  • 1 cup purple rice flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Time

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20-30 minutes
  • Total Time: 30-40 minutes
  • Servings: 6-8

Procedure

  1. Prepare Batter: Mix purple rice flour, sugar, coconut milk, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Steam: Pour the batter into bamboo tubes or small molds.
  3. Cook: Steam for 20-30 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Serve: Serve with grated coconut and sugar.
